Hindi Name: Amla
Sanskrit Name: Amlaki
English Name: Indian Gooseberry
Latin Name: Emblica officinalis Linn
A middle sized deciduous tree with green globose fruits
found wild throughout tropical India.
The tree is extensively cultivated these days in many parts of India for its fruits.
